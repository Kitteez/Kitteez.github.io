Dogs

Website Project, Gavin Feldman

Dogs are great animals, in terms of being good friends to us.
Personally, I own a Min Pin and an American Eskimo. Both dogs are young
and nice, and are very comforting.

Reasons why dogs are great pets

  • They're cute
  • They care about us if we treat them nice
  • We are able to play and have fun with them

Dogs and people

Just like people, dogs are very social creatures.
They love attention and being played with, just like we do.
This is just more reason to have one, as they're great friends!
